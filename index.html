What is hasthanirmita?

hasthanirmita simply means HASTHA- Hand, NIRMIT(a)- Made.
This brand resemble women and the weavers of this country.This brand aims at changing the image of homegrown saree from boring to contemporary vougue, which can resemble as power-dressing for every generation and not just seen as old school midlife staples.
